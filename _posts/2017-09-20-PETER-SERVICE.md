**Title**: Our experience in DPI*GRID

**PETER-SERVICE DPI GRID** is a hardware-software system based on Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) technology, which allows analyzing not only the headers of IP-packets, but also the contents of a whole series of packets for detecting characteristic identifying features.

**Classification of IP traffic**:
- By types of network protocols;
- By ports;
- By IP addresses;
- By subnet masks.

**10Gb/s * N**

Up to 28 million packets per second per server
